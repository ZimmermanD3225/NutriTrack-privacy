NutriTrack AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

NutriTrack AI collects the following information to provide personalized nutrition coaching services:

  • Nutrition Data: Food items, calorie counts, macronutrients, and meal information you enter
  • Health Metrics: Weight, height, age, and fitness goals you provide
  • Apple Health Data: With your explicit permission, we may read and write nutrition data to Apple Health
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the app to improve our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered nutrition coaching through Claude AI by Anthropic
  • Track your nutrition goals and progress
  • Generate personalized meal suggestions and nutrition advice
  • Sync your nutrition data with Apple Health (only with your permission)
  • Improve our app's features and user experience

3. Apple Health Integration

With your permission, NutriTrack AI can:

  • Read nutrition data from Apple Health to provide comprehensive tracking
  • Write nutrition data to Apple Health to keep all your health data in one place
  • You can revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in your device settings

4. Claude AI Processing

Your nutrition questions and data are processed by Claude AI (by Anthropic) to provide personalized coaching:

  • Your questions are sent securely to Claude AI for analysis
  • Claude AI processes your data according to Anthropic's privacy policy
  • We do not store conversation history beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • Your personal data is not used to train AI models

5. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Secure storage practices for any locally stored data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No sharing of personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

6. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is:

  • Stored locally on your device for offline access
  • Synced with Apple Health if you grant permission
  • Retained only as long as you use the app
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app or request deletion

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data within the app
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of Apple Health integration at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

NutriTrack AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
